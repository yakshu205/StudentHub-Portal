Frequently Asked Questions

StudentHub Portal

1. How do I register on StudentHub?

Click on the Register page, fill in your details, and submit the registration form.

2. How do I login?

Enter your registered email and password on the Login page.

3. Can I edit my profile?

Yes. Go to the Profile page and click the Edit Profile button.

4. How can I check my attendance?

Login to your Dashboard and open the Attendance section.

5. Where can I see upcoming events?

Visit the Events page to view all upcoming university events.

6. I forgot my password. What should I do?

Contact the administrator or use the Forgot Password option (if available).

7. How can I contact StudentHub?

Visit the Contact page or email us at studenthub@gmail.com.
